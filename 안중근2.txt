유묵을 통해 볼 때 안중근은 높은 식견과 넓은 세계관을 겸비한 지식인임을 알 수 있

In the end, we are concerned about Ahn's attitude of honoring teacher. By 
studing his philosophy of honoring teacher it is possible to judge  the course 
of his lofty character building as well as academic branch he belonged to,  His 
excellent calligraphy suggests that Ahn is an intelligent man with high insight 
and a wide outlook. Therefore, It is admitted that Ahn recieved high quality 
education, and it is not appropriate that he grew up and got educated by his own 
effort without teachers, which has been claimed by some researchers

고증을 통해 볼 때 안중근은 화서학파의 세 스승으로부터 전통적인 깊은 학문을 터득
하였다. 고석노, 이상설, 류인석이 그의 스승이다. 따라서 그의 사승관계에서 볼 때 
그는 화서학맥의 연원이다. 이에 더하여 천주교의 교리를 연구하는 과정에서 서양의 
문물을 익히고 세상의 견문을 넓혀 그의 탁월한 세계관이 형성된 것이다.

When viewed from historical sources, Ahn learned traditional classics deeply 
from the 3 teachers of Hwaseo school. They were Seokno Ko, Sangseol Lee, and 
Inseok Rhy.  Thus his acdemic origin was fron Hwaseo school. In addition, during 
study of Catholic doctrine , He learned the western culture and widened the 
knowledge of the world and formed his excellent worldview.

이와 같이 나라와 겨레를 위해 훌륭한 일을 수행할 수 있는 안중근의 역할에는 그의 
선천적 영민함과 후천적으로 터득한 식견과 세계관의 힘이 컸을 것이다."
